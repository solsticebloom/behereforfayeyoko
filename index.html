Faye Peraya Malisorn, known professionally as Supaporn Malisorn. She is a well-known Thai actress, model, and former beauty queen. She gained widespread recognition after winning Miss Grand Thailand in 2016, which launched her career in the entertainment industry. Born on October 14, 1994, in Prachin Buri, Thailand, she initially dreamed of becoming a teacher but later pursued a career in acting after achieving success in beauty pageants. Faye signed a contract with Channel 7 in 2017, which significantly boosted her profile in Thai television. Some of her notable roles include playing Khun Nueng in "Blank the Series", a popular Thai GL drama that has captivated audiences with its heartfelt storyline.

Career Highlights

  • Debut: Faye made her acting debut in the television series Mussaya (Thai: มัสยา) in 2017.
  • Breakthrough Role: Her role in Blank The Series where she played the character Khun Nueng. This role significantly boosted her popularity, making her a well-known figure in the Thai entertainment industry. The success of "Blank The Series," especially its heartfelt storyline involving her character, played a key role in elevating her career, leading to further opportunities and recognition both in Thailand and internationally.
  • Awards: Faye has won several awards, including Miss Grand Thailand 2016 and Beauty Queen of the Year 20th Komchadluek Awards 2024.
  • Recent Work: Recently, she starred in Blank The Series, which was a major hit both commercially and critically.

Personal Achievements

  • Education: She holds a degree in Bachelor of Business Administration from Ramkhamhaeng University.
  • Personal Interests: Faye enjoys painting, playing guitar, and playing badminton in her spare time.

Company

Faye is associated with NineStarStudiosTH, a renowned production company known for its high-quality content and strong female leadership.

AMOR BOUTIQUE SALON

Yoko Apasra Lertprasert, known professionally as Apasra Lertprasert, was born in Bangkok, Thailand on July 19, 2001. She has one older sister, actress Neko Naerunchara Lertprasert. Apasra Lertprasert started acting at the age of two, showcasing her natural talent and love for the craft. She was introduced as a former member of the first generation of The Glass Girls on July 15, 2019. She attended Southland Girls High School in New Zealand before going on to study at Stamford International University.

Career Highlights

  • Debut: Yoko made her acting debut in the television movies Ladda Land. in 2011.
  • Breakthrough Role: Her role in Blank The Series where she played the character Anueng. This popular role greatly boosted her profile in the Thai entertainment industry and opened up more opportunities for her both in Thailand and internationally.
  • Recent Work: Recently, she starred in Blank The Series, which was a major hit both commercially and critically.

Personal Achievements

  • Education: She holds a degree in Bachelor of Communication Arts from Stamford International University.
  • Personal Interests: Yoko enjoys painting, dancing, and singing in her spare time.
